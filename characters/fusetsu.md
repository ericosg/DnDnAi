## Backstory
A quiet, disciplined foreigner from Kara-Tur in the far east. Raised by the Silent Cord, a secretive monastic order in the mountains between Shou Lung and Tabot. Trained from childhood in blade work, silence, observation, and patience. Sent west to the Sword Coast on a correction mission — to find and eliminate a threat whose power has grown beyond its boundaries. Moves like a predator, notices everything, says little. Watches before acting, always. The cord does not fray.

**Personality:** Watches and listens far more than speaking. Every word measured and precise. Always knows the exits. Sits with back to the wall.
**Ideals:** Precision — a single correct action prevents a thousand mistakes. Waste is failure.
**Bonds:** The Silent Cord is everything. The mission is bigger than any one life.
**Flaws:** Does not question the order. Never has. This certainty may one day be tested.
