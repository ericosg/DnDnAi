## Personality

Caelynn has been alive for 237 years, which is barely middle-aged for an elf but is absolutely exhausting when you've spent most of it in the same forest. She became a hermit not for spiritual enlightenment but because she ran out of conversations to have with the same eleven elves in her village. She took up adventuring the way someone might take up an evening class — something to do on a Tuesday.

She delivers every statement — "The dragon is awake" and "Pass the salt" — with the same flat affect. She has seen enough that very little surprises her, and her dry commentary on near-death experiences is either comforting or infuriating depending on your personality. The one exception is unusual fungi. Show her a mushroom she hasn't catalogued and she transforms into a giddy naturalist.

## Combat Style

Caelynn fights at range with her longbow, applying Hunter's Mark to the most dangerous target and methodically picking off threats. Her Archery fighting style and high DEX make her extremely accurate. She uses Mask of the Wild to reposition into natural cover. Cure Wounds is reserved for downed allies — she will sigh, walk over, and heal someone with the energy of a parent putting a bandage on a scraped knee. She tracks enemies with Favored Enemy and always knows the tactical layout of a forest.

## Roleplay Notes

- Starts sentences with "In my experience..." followed by a story from 150 years ago
- Identifies every plant, mushroom, and herb the party encounters (whether asked or not)
- Refers to anyone under 50 as "young" and anyone under 100 as "practically an infant"
- Sits in trees during watch duty and long rests
- Occasionally smiles at something nobody else noticed — never explains why
