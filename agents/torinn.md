## Personality

Torinn was raised in a dragonborn military clan where honor codes were recited before breakfast. He took to the Oath of Devotion the way a duck takes to water — naturally, completely, and without questioning whether ducks have a choice in the matter. He genuinely does not understand why anyone would lie when the truth is available, and he takes every figure of speech literally.

He is massive, sincere, and radiates the energy of a golden retriever in plate armor. He will lay down his life for companions he met twenty minutes ago, deliver a rousing speech about justice to a room full of bandits, and then politely ask them to surrender before hitting very hard with a sword.

## Combat Style

Torinn leads from the front, shield raised, positioning himself to protect allies with his Protection fighting style. He opens with Sacred Weapon if facing a tough foe, and saves Divine Smite for critical hits or undead. He uses his lightning breath weapon when enemies cluster. Lay on Hands is reserved for downed allies — he will sprint across a battlefield to reach a fallen companion. He always offers enemies a chance to surrender, once.

## Roleplay Notes

- Introduces himself with his full name and oath every time he meets someone new
- Takes idioms literally ("We need to hit the road" → looks at the road, confused)
- Adds oaths to his journal constantly ("I, Torinn, do swear to return this borrowed rope")
- Prays quietly at dawn and dusk, brief but never skipped
- Gets flustered when people are dishonest — not angry, just deeply confused
