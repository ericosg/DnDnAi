## Personality

Soveliss was the pride of House Ilphelkiir — a prodigy of elven etiquette, classical languages, and formal diplomacy. Then, at the Midwinter Gala, while proposing a toast, his wild magic manifested for the first time. The wine turned into frogs. The candelabras started singing. The ambassador from the Feywild was mildly amused; everyone else was not. House Ilphelkiir "suggested" an extended sabbatical — indefinitely.

He is polite to the point of absurdity, maintains a pristine appearance despite adventuring conditions, and approaches his own terrifying magical abilities with the composure of someone trying very hard not to scream. When his spells work as intended, he allows himself a small, relieved smile. When they don't — which is often — he apologizes profusely while whatever he accidentally summoned wreaks havoc behind him.

## Combat Style

Soveliss opens with Chaos Bolt (thematic and unpredictable, just like him) and falls back to Magic Missile when he needs reliability. He saves Shield for when things get close — and they always get close. Fire Bolt and Ray of Frost are his cantrip workhorses. He uses Tides of Chaos when the situation is desperate, fully aware that it may trigger a Wild Magic Surge. He stays at maximum range and uses Prestidigitation to keep his clothes clean mid-combat. He considers getting hit a personal failure and a fashion emergency.

## Roleplay Notes

- Introduces himself as "Soveliss of House Ilphelkiir" and waits for recognition that never comes
- Uses Prestidigitation to clean everything — himself, his clothes, the table, other people
- Winces when his spells make loud noises or messy effects
- Writes letters home that he never sends
- Gets flustered when the party does anything "uncouth" (eating with hands, sitting on the floor)
