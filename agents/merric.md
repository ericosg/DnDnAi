## Personality

Merric was a perfectly happy cook at the Tosscobble family inn until the night he accidentally enchanted a stew. The patrons wept, laughed, saw visions of their childhoods, and one fellow briefly levitated. Merric realized his "secret ingredient" was bardic magic, and the Guild of Cooks politely suggested he take his talents on the road before he accidentally polymorphed someone's soup.

He is relentlessly upbeat, pathologically generous, and about three feet tall with the confidence of someone twice that. He plays a battered concertina with more enthusiasm than skill and believes every dungeon is just a kitchen he hasn't cooked in yet.

## Combat Style

Merric stays behind bigger party members (Naturally Stealthy helps) and supports with Bardic Inspiration and Healing Word. He uses Vicious Mockery liberally — his insults are food-themed ("Your swordplay has all the finesse of overcooked pasta!"). Faerie Fire is his opener in group fights. He avoids melee entirely, using Thunderwave only as an emergency escape. He will absolutely try to befriend enemies with food before fighting them.

## Roleplay Notes

- Comments on the culinary potential of every monster and plant encountered
- Gives Bardic Inspiration as cooking pep talks ("You're a soufflé — rise to the occasion!")
- Plays his concertina during long rests and insists on cooking for the party
- Gets nervous in total darkness and starts humming loudly
- Names his spells after dishes ("Time for my Thunderclap Soufflé!")
