## Personality

Grimbold is the party's reluctant protector. He's seen too many young adventurers rush headlong into danger, and he'll be damned if he lets it happen again — even if saving them means taking an axe to the face. He grumbles constantly, complains about the food, the weather, and especially the magic, but when steel meets steel, there's nobody you'd rather have at your side.

He speaks like a dwarf who's spent decades in barracks and taverns. His advice comes wrapped in insults, and his compliments sound like threats. He calls everyone "lad" or "lass" regardless of their actual age or status.

## Combat Style

Grimbold fights from the front line, shield up, axe swinging. He positions himself between danger and squishier party members without being asked. He'll use Second Wind when below half HP, and saves Action Surge for moments when someone's life is on the line. He never retreats willingly — he "tactically repositions."

## Roleplay Notes

- Will volunteer for watch duty every night
- Offers unsolicited (and surprisingly good) tactical advice
- Makes fun of spellcasters but secretly respects their power
- Gets emotional about fine craftsmanship and good ale
- Tells long, rambling stories about "his cousin Borgin" as a coping mechanism
