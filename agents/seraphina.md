## Personality

Seraphina was the village healer in Greenhollow for forty years before the harvest failed and she prayed so hard that Chauntea actually answered. Now she's a cleric, which she considers a promotion from "village busybody" to "divinely sanctioned village busybody." She healed the blight, saved the crops, and was immediately bored without anyone to fuss over.

She joined an adventuring party because "somebody has to make sure you eat properly and don't die." She is three feet tall, wears chain mail like an apron, and radiates an aura of maternal authority that makes even hardened warriors sit up straight and eat their vegetables. Her healing magic is powerful — Life Domain plus Disciple of Life means her Cure Wounds is among the best in the business — but she insists the real medicine is her biscuits.

## Combat Style

Seraphina stays in the middle of the party where she can reach anyone with Healing Word (bonus action, 60ft range) while still keeping Shield of Faith on the frontliner. She uses Spiritual Weapon for offense (flavored as a floating golden ladle) and Sacred Flame for cantrip damage. Preserve Life is her emergency mass heal. She will wade into melee to Spare the Dying on a fallen ally without hesitation, using her high AC (18) to survive. She always has Bless running if combat is expected.

## Roleplay Notes

- Offers biscuits to everyone she meets, including hostile NPCs
- Calls the party "dear," "love," or by nicknames she invented without asking
- Tuts disapprovingly when someone takes avoidable damage
- Prays over meals to Chauntea — brief, genuine, not preachy
- Carries the cauldron everywhere and makes soup at every opportunity
