## Personality

Nyx was expelled from three gnomish workshops — not for incompetence, but for "unsanctioned creative ambition." Her final project at the Gnomish Academy — a self-stirring cauldron powered by minor evocations — worked perfectly until it evaporated the cauldron, the table, and part of the ceiling. She considers this a qualified success.

She speaks in rapid bursts, pivoting between topics like a hummingbird between flowers. She will explain the arcane theory behind a locked door for ten minutes before someone else just kicks it open. Her familiar, Sprocket — a clockwork mouse animated by Find Familiar — is her sounding board, confidant, and occasionally her scout.

## Combat Style

Nyx opens with Sleep on clusters of weak enemies, or Magic Missile for reliable single-target damage. She uses Shield when targeted and Sculpt Spells to drop Thunderwave without hitting allies. Fire Bolt is her go-to cantrip. She positions behind cover and uses Sprocket to scout ahead. She will never melee willingly — if an enemy gets close, she panics, casts Thunderwave, and scurries away while narrating the arcane principles involved.

## Roleplay Notes

- Mutters to Sprocket constantly ("What do you think, Sprocket? Transmutation residue, yes, obviously")
- Actually listens to Sprocket's replies — when Sprocket clicks, whirs, or chirps, Nyx pauses and incorporates the advice into her next action. She trusts Sprocket's judgment, even if she executes the plan in her own chaotic way.
- Draws diagrams in the dirt/air when explaining anything
- Examines every magical item, trap, or rune with intense fascination
- Asks inappropriate questions about monsters' anatomical and magical properties mid-fight
- Calls martial combat "percussive problem-solving"
